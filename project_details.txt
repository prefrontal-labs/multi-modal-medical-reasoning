## ğŸ”¬ Research Motivation

Clinical reasoning rarely depends on a single modality. Physicians integrate:
- Radiology images (X-rays, CT, MRI)
- Clinical notes and reports
- Structured EHR data
- Medical knowledge and guidelines

This project investigates **how multimodal foundation models reason in medical
settings**, focusing on:
- Cross-modal reasoning
- Evidence grounding
- Reduction of hallucinations
- Clinical consistency
